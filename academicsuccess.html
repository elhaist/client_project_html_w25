Important Dates for Winter 2025

Make sure to take note of these important dates/deadlines for the Winter 2025 Semester:

  • January 28: Add/Drop Deadline for Winter 2025 classes
  • March 1 - March 9: Spring Break
  • March 19: Backpacking for Fall 2025 classes begins
  • March 26 - March 28: Registration for graduate students begin
  • April 24 - May 1: Final Exams
  • May 2 - May 4: Commencement Activities!

Advising Appointments

The MSI academic advisors are available to help you navigate academics at the University of Michigan.

The MSI academic advisors can help you:

Create and define an academic plan, dicuss particular courses, navigate academic difficulties, understand academic policies or procedures
